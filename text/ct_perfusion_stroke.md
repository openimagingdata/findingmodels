# Ct perfusion stroke—`OIFM_CDE_000224`

CT Perfusion Stroke

**Codes:** RADELEMENT RDES224 ct perfusion stroke

## Attributes

### Intracerebral circulation involved—`OIFMA_CDE_001458`

Which circulation has a perfusion abnormality?  
*(Select up to 8)*
- **right middle cerebral artery**  
- **left middle cerebral artery**  
- **right anterior cerebral artery**  
- **left anterior cerebral artery**  
- **right posterior cerebral artery**  
- **left posterior cerebral artery**  
- **unknown**  
- **indeterminate**  

### Perfusion mismatch—`OIFMA_CDE_001459`

DIscordant or equivalent volumes of involved penumbra and ischemic core.  Expressed as none, left MCA or right MCA territory.  
*(Select one)*

- **absent**: Select if there is no perfusion deficit or if there is matching size/territory of ischemic core and penumbra.  
- **present**: Ischemic penumbra exceeds ischemic core in the right MCA territory.  
- **indeterminate**  
- **unknown**  

### Ischemic core volume—`OIFMA_CDE_001460`

The volume of brain on CBF maps with less than 30% cerebral blood flow indicating ischemic core  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 500  
Unit: cc

### Ischemic penumbra volume—`OIFMA_CDE_001461`

Volume of brain where the maximum transit time exceeds 6 seconds indicative of ischemic penumbra  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 500  
Unit: cc

### Mismatch volume—`OIFMA_CDE_001462`

The difference in brain volume between the ischemic penumbra (Tmax > 6 sec) and the ischemic core (CBF <30%)  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 500  
Unit: cc

### Poor collateral perfusion volume—`OIFMA_CDE_001995`

(Tmax > 10 s)   
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 100  
Unit: ml

### Hypoperfusion index—`OIFMA_CDE_001996`

(Tmax > 10 s/Tmax > 6 s)   
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 100  
Unit: unit

### Mismatch ratio—`OIFMA_CDE_001997`

Mismatch ratio is defined as the perfusion deficit divide by the infarct core volume (=volume of tissue with Tmax >6 s/volume of tissue with rCBF <30%).  
Mininum: 0  
Maximum: 100  
Unit: unit
