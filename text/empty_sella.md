# Empty sella—`OIFM_OIDM_132795`

**Synonyms:** empty pituitary fossa, CSF-filled sella

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

CSF filling and expansion of the sella with flattening or nonvisualization of the pituitary gland, often seen as an incidental sellar finding on CT or MRI.
