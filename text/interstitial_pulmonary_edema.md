# Interstitial pulmonary edema—`OIFM_OIDM_651696`

**Synonyms:** interstitial edema, pulmonary interstitial edema

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Fluid accumulation in the pulmonary interstitium on chest radiograph, manifesting as Kerley lines, peribronchial cuffing, and septal thickening, typically preceding alveolar edema.
