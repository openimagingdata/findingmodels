# Widened cardiomediastinal silhouette—`OIFM_OIDM_698793`

**Synonyms:** widening of cardiomediastinal silhouette, broad cardiomediastinal silhouette, widened mediastinal silhouette

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased width of the combined cardiac and mediastinal shadow on chest radiograph, which may indicate cardiomegaly, pericardial effusion, mediastinal mass, or technical factors such as AP projection.
