# Aortic arch variant—`OIFM_OIDM_186538`

**Synonyms:** aortic arch variant, variant of the aortic arch anatomy, anatomic variant of the aortic arch

Anatomic variation in the morphology or branching pattern of the aortic arch identified on imaging. It is typically incidental and without hemodynamic consequence, though certain configurations may have clinical relevance (example: 'aortic arch variant').
