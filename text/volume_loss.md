# Volume loss—`OIFM_OIDM_071216`

**Synonyms:** pulmonary volume loss, lung volume loss

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Reduction in the volume of a lung or portion of lung on chest radiograph, manifested by displacement of fissures, mediastinal shift, diaphragm elevation, or crowding of pulmonary vessels.
