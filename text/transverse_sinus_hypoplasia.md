# Transverse sinus hypoplasia—`OIFM_OIDM_381215`

**Synonyms:** hypoplasia of the transverse sinus, transverse dural sinus hypoplasia, underdeveloped transverse sinus, lesser caliber transverse sinus, transverse venous sinus hypoplasia

Congenital or developmental underdevelopment of the transverse dural venous sinus, producing a reduced caliber of the transverse sinus. This is commonly an incidental anatomic variant on MR venography/CT venography and may be misinterpreted as transverse sinus thrombosis if not correlated with clinical and imaging features.
