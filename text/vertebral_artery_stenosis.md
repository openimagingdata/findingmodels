# Vertebral artery stenosis—`OIFM_OIDM_488023`

**Synonyms:** vertebral artery stenosis, VA stenosis, stenosis of the vertebral artery

Vertebral artery stenosis is narrowing of the vertebral artery, which can compromise posterior circulation perfusion. Radiologic assessment may report percent luminal stenosis and mention potential clinical consequences such as vertebrobasilar insufficiency or posterior circulation ischemia/stroke risk.
