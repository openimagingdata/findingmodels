# Globular cardiac silhouette—`OIFM_OIDM_840763`

**Synonyms:** water bottle heart, flask-shaped heart, globular heart

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, cardiac

Rounded, flask-shaped enlargement of the cardiac silhouette on chest radiograph, classically associated with pericardial effusion.
