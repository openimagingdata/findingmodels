# Oroantral fistula—`OIFM_OIDM_089707`

**Synonyms:** OAF, oro-antral fistula, odontogenic sinus fistula, dental-sinus fistula

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

An epithelialized abnormal communication between the oral cavity and maxillary sinus, often related to dental extraction, infection, trauma, or surgery.
