# Burr hole—`OIFM_OIDM_687433`

**Synonyms:** burr hole, burr holes, surgical burr hole, calvarial burr hole, trephination site

A burr hole is a surgically created opening in the calvarium for neurosurgical access or decompression. On imaging, it appears as a small, round skull defect with well-defined margins; postoperative changes may include surrounding bone remodeling.
