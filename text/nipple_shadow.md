# Nipple shadow—`OIFM_OIDM_424230`

**Synonyms:** nipple projection, nipple artifact

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Soft tissue density on chest radiograph corresponding to the projected nipple, which may mimic a pulmonary nodule.
