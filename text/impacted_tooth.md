# Impacted tooth—`OIFM_OIDM_103644`

**Synonyms:** unerupted tooth, embedded tooth, impacted molar

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A tooth that has failed to erupt normally and remains partially or completely embedded within bone or soft tissue.
