# Diaphragmatic eventration—`OIFM_OIDM_483201`

**Synonyms:** eventration of hemidiaphragm, eventration of the diaphragm, diaphragm eventration

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, diaphragm

Abnormal elevation of a portion or all of a hemidiaphragm due to congenital absence or thinning of diaphragmatic muscle, resulting in a thin membranous sheet replacing normal muscle.
