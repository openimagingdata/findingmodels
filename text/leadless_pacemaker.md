# Leadless pacemaker—`OIFM_OIDM_732956`

**Synonyms:** leadless cardiac pacemaker, Micra device, Micra pacemaker

**Tags:** chest, XR, device, finding

Self-contained pacemaker implanted directly in the cardiac chamber without transvenous leads, visible as a small capsule.
