# White matter disease—`OIFM_OIDM_106672`

**Synonyms:** leukoencephalopathy, leukoencephalopathies, leukoaraiosis

White matter disease refers to non-specific abnormalities of the cerebral white matter, typically demonstrated as T2/FLAIR hyperintensities on MRI that may reflect demyelination, gliosis, or chronic small vessel ischemia. It is an imaging finding with a broad differential, commonly associated with aging and vascular risk factors.
