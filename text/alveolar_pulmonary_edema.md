# Alveolar pulmonary edema—`OIFM_OIDM_350759`

**Synonyms:** alveolar edema, florid pulmonary edema, frank pulmonary edema

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Fluid filling of the alveolar spaces on chest radiograph, manifesting as bilateral airspace opacities, air bronchograms, and often a perihilar or dependent distribution.
