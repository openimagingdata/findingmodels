# Retained contrast—`OIFM_OIDM_945616`

**Synonyms:** residual contrast from prior study, contrast staining, retained iodinated contrast

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Residual iodinated contrast material visible from prior contrast administration, which may mimic or obscure acute hemorrhage or enhancement on CT.
