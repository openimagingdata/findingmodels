# Subependymal edema—`OIFM_OIDM_762623`

**Synonyms:** subependymal edema, edema of the subependymal region, subependymal region edema

Edema localized to the subependymal region along the ventricular lining. The finding is non-specific and should be interpreted in the clinical context with other imaging features.
