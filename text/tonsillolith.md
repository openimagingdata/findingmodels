# Tonsillolith—`OIFM_OIDM_661237`

**Synonyms:** tonsillar calcification, tonsil stone, tonsillar concretion, palatine tonsil calcification

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A calcified concretion within a tonsillar crypt, typically seen on CT as a small calcification in the palatine tonsil region.
