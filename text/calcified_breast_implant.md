# Calcified breast implant—`OIFM_OIDM_713410`

**Synonyms:** breast implant calcification, calcified breast implant capsule

**Tags:** chest, XR, soft tissue, finding

Calcification of the capsule surrounding a breast implant, visible as curvilinear density on chest radiograph.
