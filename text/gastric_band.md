# Gastric band—`OIFM_OIDM_537627`

**Synonyms:** Lap-Band, adjustable gastric band

**Tags:** chest, XR, device, finding

Adjustable band placed around the gastric cardia for bariatric surgery, visible as a radiopaque ring in the left upper quadrant.
