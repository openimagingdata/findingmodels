# Apical pleuroparenchymal scarring—`OIFM_OIDM_412273`

**Synonyms:** apical cap, apical caps, apical scarring, apical pleural thickening, apical fibrosis, biapical thickening, biapical scarring

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Fibrous thickening and scarring involving the apical pleura and adjacent lung parenchyma, commonly bilateral, seen as apical caps on chest radiograph.
