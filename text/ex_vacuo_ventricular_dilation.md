# Ex vacuo ventricular dilation—`OIFM_OIDM_138708`

**Synonyms:** ex vacuo dilatation, compensatory ventriculomegaly, passive ventricular enlargement

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Compensatory ventricular enlargement secondary to brain parenchymal volume loss, rather than obstructed CSF flow.
