# Hiatal hernia—`OIFM_OIDM_907498`

**Synonyms:** hiatus hernia, diaphragmatic hiatal hernia

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Herniation of abdominal contents (typically stomach) through the esophageal hiatus of the diaphragm, visible on chest radiograph as a retrocardiac soft tissue density often with an air-fluid level.
