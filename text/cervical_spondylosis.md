# Cervical spondylosis—`OIFM_OIDM_371812`

**Synonyms:** cervical spine degeneration, degenerative changes of the cervical spine, degenerative cervical spondylosis, degenerative cervical spine disease, degenerative changes in the cervical spine

Cervical spondylosis denotes degenerative changes of the cervical spine, including disc space narrowing, osteophyte formation, facet arthropathy, and endplate sclerosis. These changes may contribute to neural foraminal or central canal stenosis and can be associated with cervical radiculopathy or myelopathy.
