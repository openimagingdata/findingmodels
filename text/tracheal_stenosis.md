# Tracheal stenosis—`OIFM_OIDM_620490`

**Synonyms:** tracheal narrowing, stenosis of trachea, tracheal stricture

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Focal or diffuse narrowing of the tracheal lumen visible on chest radiograph, which may result from prior intubation, surgery, inflammation, or neoplasm.
