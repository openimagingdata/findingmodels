# Middle meningeal artery embolization—`OIFM_OIDM_723535`

**Synonyms:** MMA embolization, embolization of the middle meningeal artery

A minimally invasive endovascular procedure in which embolic material is delivered through a catheter to occlude the middle meningeal artery, reducing dural blood flow. It is performed to manage or reduce recurrence of chronic subdural hematoma and other dural vascular pathologies.
