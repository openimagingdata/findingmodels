# Soft tissue abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_131313`

**Synonyms:** soft tissue abnormality, soft tissue lesion, extraosseous abnormality, extraosseous lesion

**Tags:** soft tissue, musculoskeletal, lesion, mass

A lesion or abnormality located outside osseous structures, typically within adjacent soft tissue or non-bony compartments; may be described as an extraosseous lesion or extraosseous mass depending on imaging characteristics.
