# Widening of rib interspaces—`OIFM_OIDM_594364`

**Synonyms:** widened intercostal spaces, widened rib spaces

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

Increased distance between adjacent ribs, suggesting hyperinflation, mass effect, or prior surgery.
