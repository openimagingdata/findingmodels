# Depressed hemidiaphragm—`OIFM_OIDM_432021`

**Synonyms:** depression of hemidiaphragm, inferiorly displaced hemidiaphragm, depressed diaphragm

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, diaphragm

Inferior displacement of a hemidiaphragm below its normal position, typically caused by hyperinflation, large pleural effusion, or tension pneumothorax.
