# Abnormal left paraspinal line—`OIFM_OIDM_568158`

**Synonyms:** left paraspinal line abnormality, widened left paraspinal stripe

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Displacement or widening of the left paraspinal line on chest radiograph, which may indicate aortic pathology, vertebral body fracture, lymphadenopathy, or paraspinal mass.
