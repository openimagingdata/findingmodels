# Calvarial fracture—`OIFM_OIDM_678064`

**Synonyms:** calvarial fracture, skull fracture, cranial vault fracture, cranial fracture, calvarial bone fracture

A fracture of the skull vault (calvaria). It is typically linear but may be depressed; may be associated with intracranial injury depending on mechanism and force.
