# Pulmonary scar—`OIFM_OIDM_745898`

**Synonyms:** pulmonary scarring, lung scar, scar tissue in the lung, parenchymal scar

Pulmonary scar denotes residual scarring of the lung parenchyma, typically reflecting prior inflammation or injury. Radiographically it may appear as linear or band-like markings, often subpleural or along fissures; correlate with history of prior infection, inflammation, trauma, or surgery.
