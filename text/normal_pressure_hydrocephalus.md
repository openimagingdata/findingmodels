# Normal pressure hydrocephalus—`OIFM_OIDM_559938`

**Synonyms:** NPH

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A syndrome of gait disturbance, cognitive decline, and urinary incontinence with ventriculomegaly and normal opening pressure on lumbar puncture.
