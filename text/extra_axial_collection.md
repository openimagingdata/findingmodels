# Extra-axial collection—`OIFM_OIDM_064727`

**Synonyms:** extra-axial collection, extradural hematoma, epidural hematoma, epidural collection, subdural hematoma, subdural collection, subarachnoid hemorrhage, extraparenchymal collection

An extra-axial collection denotes a fluid or blood collection located outside the brain parenchyma, within the intracranial spaces such as epidural, subdural, or subarachnoid compartments. It encompasses subtypes including epidural hematoma, subdural hematoma, and subarachnoid hemorrhage; the term may be used in reports to denote this broad category before specifying the exact location or etiology.
