# Lateral ventricle effacement—`OIFM_OIDM_372233`

**Synonyms:** lateral ventricle effacement, effacement of the lateral ventricles, ventricular effacement, LVE

Lateral ventricle effacement refers to the compression or obliteration of the normal lateral ventricular margins due to adjacent mass effect, edema, or space-occupying pathology, resulting in reduced ventricular CSF spaces. Original phrasing: 'lateral ventricle effacement'.
