# Pulmonary mass—`OIFM_OIDM_135648`

**Synonyms:** lung mass lesion, intrapulmonary mass, lung mass, mass

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A pulmonary opacity greater than 3 cm in diameter on chest radiograph, raising concern for primary lung malignancy, metastasis, or other solid lesion.
