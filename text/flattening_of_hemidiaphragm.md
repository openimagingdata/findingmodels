# Flattening of hemidiaphragm—`OIFM_OIDM_716965`

**Synonyms:** flattened hemidiaphragm, flat hemidiaphragm, diaphragmatic flattening

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Loss of the normal dome shape of a hemidiaphragm, appearing flat or inverted on chest radiograph, typically indicating hyperinflation or increased intrathoracic pressure.
