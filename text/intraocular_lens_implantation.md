# Intraocular lens implantation—`OIFM_OIDM_994890`

**Synonyms:** lens replacement, lens implant, intraocular lens prosthesis, IOL implantation, IOL prosthesis

Presence of an intraocular lens (IOL) prosthesis in the posterior chamber consistent with prior cataract extraction and lens replacement. The term 'lens replacement' may be used in radiology reports as an example of this finding.
