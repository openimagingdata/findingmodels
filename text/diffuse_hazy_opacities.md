# Diffuse hazy opacities—`OIFM_OIDM_923053`

**Synonyms:** diffuse haziness, bilateral hazy opacities, diffuse ground-glass opacity

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Widespread, ill-defined, ground-glass-like opacification of the lungs on chest radiograph, suggesting edema, hemorrhage, or early infectious/inflammatory process.
