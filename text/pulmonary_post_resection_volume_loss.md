# Pulmonary post-resection volume loss—`OIFM_OIDM_392629`

**Synonyms:** postsurgical volume loss, post-lobectomy volume loss, surgical volume loss, post-resection volume loss

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Reduction in lung volume following surgical resection (lobectomy, segmentectomy, or wedge resection), visible as ipsilateral volume loss with compensatory changes on chest radiograph.
