# Sulcal effacement—`OIFM_OIDM_926943`

**Synonyms:** effacement of the cerebral sulci, sulcal flattening, loss of sulcal markings

Loss or attenuation of normal cerebral sulci with effacement of the sulcal markings due to brain parenchymal swelling. This finding commonly indicates cerebral edema and may reflect increased intracranial pressure or mass effect.
