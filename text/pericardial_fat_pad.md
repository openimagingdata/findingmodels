# Pericardial fat pad—`OIFM_OIDM_803542`

**Synonyms:** epicardial fat pad, paracardiac fat pad, cardiophrenic fat pad, mediastinal fat pad

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Fat deposition along the pericardium and mediastinum visible on chest radiograph, typically in the cardiophrenic angles, which is a normal variant but may mimic mass or effusion.
