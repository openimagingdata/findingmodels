# Pulmonary hypoinflation—`OIFM_OIDM_378230`

**Synonyms:** decreased lung volumes, reduced lung volumes, small lung volumes, hypoventilation, poor inspiratory volume, low lung volumes

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Reduced lung volume on chest radiograph, manifested by elevated diaphragms and crowded lung markings, which may be due to poor inspiratory effort, restrictive disease, or postoperative state.
