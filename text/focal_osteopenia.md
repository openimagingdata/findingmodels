# Focal osteopenia—`OIFM_OIDM_797563`

**Synonyms:** focal decreased bone density, regional osteopenia

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

Localized decrease in bone density in a specific region on radiograph, which may indicate disuse, inflammation, or focal pathology.
