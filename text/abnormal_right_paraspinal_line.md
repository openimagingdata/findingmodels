# Abnormal right paraspinal line—`OIFM_OIDM_980101`

**Synonyms:** right paraspinal line abnormality, widened right paraspinal stripe

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Displacement or widening of the right paraspinal line on chest radiograph, which may indicate vertebral body pathology, paraspinal mass, or pleural disease.
