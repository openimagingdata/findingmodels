# Ventriculoperitoneal shunt—`OIFM_OIDM_953974`

**Synonyms:** ventriculoperitoneal shunt apparatus, VP shunt, VPS, ventriculoperitoneal shunt system

An implanted cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) diversion device that relocates CSF from the cerebral ventricles to the peritoneal cavity to treat hydrocephalus. The system comprises an intraventricular catheter, a valve mechanism, and a distal catheter; radiology reports may use the detailed term 'ventriculoperitoneal shunt apparatus' as an example of the device's description.
