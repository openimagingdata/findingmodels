# Distension of bowel—`OIFM_OIDM_814608`

**Synonyms:** gas distended bowel, dilated bowel loops, bowel distension, dilated bowel, bowel dilation, dilation of bowel

**Tags:** chest, XR, abdomen, finding

Abnormal gaseous distension or dilation of bowel loops visible on chest radiograph, which may indicate ileus or obstruction.
