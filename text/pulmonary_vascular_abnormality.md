# Pulmonary vascular abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_113221`

**Synonyms:** abnormal pulmonary vasculature, pulmonary vascularity abnormality, pulmonary vasculature abnormality

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Any abnormal appearance of the pulmonary vasculature on chest radiograph, encompassing vascular enlargement, redistribution, diminished vessels, or other abnormal patterns.
