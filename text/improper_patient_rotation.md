# Improper patient rotation—`OIFM_OIDM_367264`

**Synonyms:** rotated image, rotated, rotation artifact, patient rotation

**Tags:** chest, XR, technique, finding

Patient is rotated relative to the X-ray beam, resulting in asymmetric appearance of mediastinal structures and potential misinterpretation.
