# Subgaleal hematoma—`OIFM_OIDM_185082`

**Synonyms:** subgaleal hemorrhage, subaponeurotic hematoma, subaponeurotic hemorrhage

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A hematoma in the potential space between the galea aponeurotica and the periosteum of the skull, typically visible as extracranial scalp swelling that can cross sutures.
