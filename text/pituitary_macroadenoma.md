# Pituitary macroadenoma—`OIFM_OIDM_913617`

**Synonyms:** macroadenoma

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A pituitary adenoma measuring at least 10 mm, typically presenting as an enlarged sellar mass that may extend into the suprasellar cistern or cavernous sinus.
