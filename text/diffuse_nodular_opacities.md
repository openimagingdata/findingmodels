# Diffuse nodular opacities—`OIFM_OIDM_204573`

**Synonyms:** disseminated nodular opacities

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Widespread small discrete rounded opacities throughout both lungs on chest radiograph, suggesting miliary disease, metastatic disease, or disseminated granulomatous infection.
