# Brain parenchymal abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_698366`

**Synonyms:** brain parenchymal abnormality, cerebral parenchymal abnormality, cerebral parenchymal lesion, brain tissue abnormality

A non-specific radiologic descriptor for an abnormality within the brain parenchyma. Additional imaging features and clinical correlation are required to determine etiology, extent, and significance; the term may be applied to focal or diffuse brain tissue abnormalities, e.g., 'brain parenchymal abnormality'.
