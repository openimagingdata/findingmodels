# Superior mediastinal widening—`OIFM_OIDM_606606`

**Synonyms:** fullness of superior mediastinum, prominent superior mediastinum, superior mediastinal fullness

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, mediastinum

Widening or increased opacity of the superior mediastinum on chest radiograph, which may indicate lymphadenopathy, vascular abnormality, or mass.
