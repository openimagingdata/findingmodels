# Soft tissue hematoma—`OIFM_OIDM_309190`

**Synonyms:** soft-tissue hematoma, subcutaneous hematoma, intramuscular hematoma, muscle hematoma

Localized collection of blood within the soft tissues, usually resulting from trauma or vessel injury. It may present as a tender, expanding swelling; imaging demonstrates a well-circumscribed collection whose signal characteristics evolve with the age of the hematoma.
