# Extracranial abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_957490`

**Synonyms:** extracranial abnormality, extracranial lesion, extra-cranial abnormality, outside-cranium abnormality

Non-specific finding referring to an abnormality located outside the cranium; definitive characterization requires correlation with imaging modality, location, size, and morphology. The phrase 'extracranial abnormality' serves as a generic descriptor and may refer to lesions in structures such as the scalp, neck, or chest outside the skull.
