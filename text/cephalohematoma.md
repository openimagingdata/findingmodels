# Cephalohematoma—`OIFM_OIDM_401242`

**Synonyms:** cephalhematoma

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A subperiosteal blood collection along the calvarium, usually confined by cranial sutures and seen as a focal extracranial scalp hematoma.
