# Subdural fluid—`OIFM_OIDM_895306`

**Synonyms:** fluid in subdural space

**Tags:** brain, intracranial, extra-axial, fluid, neuro

Fluid collection in the subdural space, commonly CSF-like, representing subdural effusion or hygroma. Typically characterized by its presence and comparison with prior imaging.
