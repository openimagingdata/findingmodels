# Organized fluid collection—`OIFM_OIDM_671373`

**Synonyms:** organized fluid collection, encapsulated fluid collection, organized effusion, encysted fluid collection

An organized fluid collection is a mature, encapsulated fluid collection with internal organization, septations or debris and a thickened wall. It may represent a chronic or organized collection such as a resolved or evolving abscess, hematoma, or seroma, depending on clinical context.
