# Pleural nodule—`OIFM_OIDM_621745`

**Synonyms:** pleural nodules, pleural-based nodule

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A discrete, rounded soft tissue density along the pleural surface on chest radiograph, which may represent mesothelioma, metastasis, or benign pleural lesion.
