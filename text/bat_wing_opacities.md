# Bat-wing opacities—`OIFM_OIDM_233741`

**Synonyms:** batwing perihilar opacities, butterfly opacities, bat wing opacities, perihilar bat-wing pattern, batwing opacities

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, lung

Symmetric bilateral perihilar opacities radiating outward from the hila in a butterfly or bat-wing configuration, typically seen on chest radiograph.
