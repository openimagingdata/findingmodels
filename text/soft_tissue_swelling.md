# Soft tissue swelling—`OIFM_OIDM_962320`

**Synonyms:** soft tissue swelling, soft-tissue swelling, soft tissue edema, edematous soft tissues, subcutaneous edema

Localized or diffuse soft tissue swelling is a non-specific radiologic finding that may reflect edema, inflammation, contusion, or hemorrhage. Correlate with clinical history and examination for possible underlying fracture or other soft-tissue injury.
