# Diffuse increased lung lucency—`OIFM_OIDM_914730`

**Synonyms:** bilateral hyperlucent lungs, diffuse hyperinflation, increased lung lucency

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Generalized increase in lung radiolucency (decreased density) on chest radiograph, commonly seen in emphysema or air trapping.
