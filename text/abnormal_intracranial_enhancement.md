# Abnormal intracranial enhancement—`OIFM_OIDM_023679`

**Synonyms:** intracranial enhancement, intracranial contrast enhancement, abnormal contrast enhancement, enhancing intracranial lesion, abnormal brain enhancement, pathologic intracranial enhancement

Focal or diffuse increased enhancement within intracranial structures on contrast-enhanced CT or MRI indicating disruption of the blood–brain barrier or abnormal vascularity; common causes include neoplasm, infection/abscess, inflammation, subacute infarction, hemorrhage, and postoperative/iatrogenic change. The original phrasing 'abnormal intracranial enhancement' is an example of this finding.
