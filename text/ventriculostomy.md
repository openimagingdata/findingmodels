# Ventriculostomy—`OIFM_OIDM_671352`

**Synonyms:** ventriculostomy, external ventricular drain, EVD, ventricular drain, intraventricular catheter, ventriculostomy catheter

Placement of a ventricular catheter for cerebrospinal fluid diversion, typically as an external ventricular drain (EVD). Radiology should document catheter tip location and trajectory, as well as the integrity of the drainage system; if a ventriculostomy is present, specify the side of approach and any malposition or over-insertion.
