# Craniotomy—`OIFM_OIDM_145443`

**Synonyms:** craniotomy, bone flap craniotomy

Craniotomy is the surgical removal of a portion of the skull (bone flap) to access intracranial structures. In radiology, the term describes postoperative skull changes, including the presence of a skull bone flap and any fixation hardware or associated postoperative findings.
