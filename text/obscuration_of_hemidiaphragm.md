# Obscuration of hemidiaphragm—`OIFM_OIDM_769251`

**Synonyms:** obscured hemidiaphragm, loss of hemidiaphragm silhouette

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, diaphragm

Loss of visualization of the hemidiaphragm silhouette, typically due to adjacent pulmonary opacity or pleural fluid.
