# Extra-axial hemorrhage—`OIFM_OIDM_077902`

**Synonyms:** extra-axial hemorrhage, extradural hemorrhage, extradural hematoma (edh)

Bleeding located outside the brain parenchyma within the skull, spanning epidural, subdural, and subarachnoid spaces. The term may be used to describe an epidural or subdural collection and can be acute or chronic.
