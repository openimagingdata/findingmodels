# Gliosis—`OIFM_OIDM_359647`

**Synonyms:** reactive gliosis, gliotic change, glial scar

Reactive proliferation of astrocytes resulting in glial scar formation following CNS injury or insult. Radiographically, gliosis presents as areas of increased signal on T2/FLAIR sequences corresponding to the site of injury and may correlate with prior infarct or trauma.
