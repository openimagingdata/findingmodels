# Internal carotid artery stenosis—`OIFM_OIDM_426788`

**Synonyms:** ICA stenosis, stenosis of the internal carotid artery, internal carotid stenosis

Localized narrowing of the internal carotid artery lumen, most commonly due to atherosclerotic plaque. The degree of stenosis is assessed on imaging and correlates with the risk of thromboembolic ischemic events.
