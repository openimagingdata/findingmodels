# Costophrenic angle blunting—`OIFM_OIDM_600445`

**Synonyms:** blunted costophrenic angle, blunting of costophrenic angle, costophrenic blunting

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Loss of the normal sharp angle between the diaphragm and chest wall on chest radiograph, typically indicating a small pleural effusion or pleural thickening.
