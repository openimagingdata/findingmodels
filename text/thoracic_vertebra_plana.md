# Thoracic vertebra plana—`OIFM_OIDM_028997`

**Synonyms:** pancake vertebra

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

Marked flattening of a thoracic vertebral body, often from compression fracture, metastasis, or eosinophilic granuloma.
