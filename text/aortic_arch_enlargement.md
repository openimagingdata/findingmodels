# Aortic arch enlargement—`OIFM_OIDM_176546`

**Synonyms:** aortic arch bulging, aortic arch prominence, aortic knob enlargement, bulging of aortic arch, bulging of the aortic arch, enlarged aortic knob, enlargement of aortic knob, prominent aortic arch, prominent aortic knob

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased prominence or size of the aortic arch and knob on chest radiograph, commonly seen with aortic tortuosity, unfolding, dilation, or aneurysm.
