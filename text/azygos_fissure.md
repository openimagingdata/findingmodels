# Azygos fissure—`OIFM_OIDM_149725`

**Synonyms:** azygos lobe fissure

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A vertically oriented curvilinear line in the right upper lung representing an accessory fissure created by the azygos vein migrating through the right upper lobe during development.
