# Abnormal aortopulmonary window contour—`OIFM_OIDM_929337`

**Synonyms:** AP window fullness, aortopulmonary window abnormality, fullness of aortopulmonary window, loss of aortopulmonary window concavity

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Abnormal fullness or convexity of the aortopulmonary window on chest radiograph, which may indicate lymphadenopathy, mass, or vascular abnormality in this mediastinal space.
