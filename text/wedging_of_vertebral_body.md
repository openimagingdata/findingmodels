# Wedging of vertebral body—`OIFM_OIDM_767171`

**Synonyms:** vertebral wedging, wedge deformity

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

Asymmetric loss of vertebral body height producing a triangular or wedge shape on lateral radiograph.
