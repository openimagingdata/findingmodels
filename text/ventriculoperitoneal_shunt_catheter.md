# Ventriculoperitoneal shunt catheter—`OIFM_OIDM_596520`

**Synonyms:** ventriculoperitoneal shunt catheter, VPS catheter, ventriculoperitoneal shunt line, VP shunt catheter, VP shunt tube

A catheter component of a ventriculoperitoneal shunt system that drains cerebrospinal fluid from the cerebral ventricle to the peritoneal cavity. Radiographic evaluation focuses on catheter trajectory, tip location, and patency, with attention to complications such as disconnection, fracture, obstruction, migration, or distal pseudocyst formation.
