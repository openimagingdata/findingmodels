# Abnormal left paratracheal stripe—`OIFM_OIDM_820347`

**Synonyms:** left paratracheal stripe abnormality, widened left paratracheal stripe

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Widening or irregularity of the left paratracheal stripe on chest radiograph, which may indicate lymphadenopathy, mediastinal mass, or other mediastinal pathology.
