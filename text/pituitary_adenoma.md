# Pituitary adenoma—`OIFM_OIDM_685143`

**Synonyms:** pituitary tumor

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A benign pituitary neuroendocrine tumor arising from the adenohypophysis, which may be seen on CT as a sellar or sellar-suprasellar mass.
