# Pulmonary vascular engorgement—`OIFM_OIDM_545430`

**Synonyms:** pulmonary vascular congestion, pulmonary venous congestion, engorgement of pulmonary vasculature, pulmonary venous hypertension

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Enlargement and increased prominence of pulmonary vessels on chest radiograph, indicating increased pulmonary blood volume as seen in fluid overload or left heart failure.
