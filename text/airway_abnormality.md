# Airway abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_449436`

**Synonyms:** tracheobronchial abnormality, airway abnormalities

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Any abnormal finding involving the tracheobronchial tree on chest radiograph, including tracheal or bronchial narrowing, deviation, dilation, or irregularity.
