# Pulmonary reticular opacities—`OIFM_OIDM_525484`

**Synonyms:** reticular opacity, diffuse reticular opacities, reticular pattern, reticular interstitial pattern, reticular opacities

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A network of linear or curvilinear opacities on chest radiograph resembling a net, typically reflecting interstitial lung disease with thickened interlobular septa or fibrosis.
