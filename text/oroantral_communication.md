# Oroantral communication—`OIFM_OIDM_193631`

**Synonyms:** OAC, oro-antral communication, post-extraction oroantral defect

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

A non-epithelialized communication or defect between the oral cavity and maxillary sinus, often after dental extraction or maxillary dental disease.
