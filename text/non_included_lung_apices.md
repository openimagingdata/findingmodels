# Non-included lung apices—`OIFM_OIDM_515194`

**Synonyms:** apices not included, cut off lung apices, clipped lung apices

**Tags:** chest, XR, technique, finding

Lung apices are not included in the image field of view, limiting evaluation of the upper lungs.
