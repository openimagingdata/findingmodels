# Dental caries—`OIFM_OIDM_547581`

**Synonyms:** tooth decay, carious tooth, cavitated tooth

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Decay or demineralization of a tooth, often visible on CT as a focal defect or lucency in the dental crown or root.
