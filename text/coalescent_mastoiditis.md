# Coalescent mastoiditis—`OIFM_OIDM_349548`

**Synonyms:** coalescent mastoid disease

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Complicated mastoiditis with destruction or coalescence of mastoid air cell septations, often with aggressive inflammatory change of the mastoid bone.
