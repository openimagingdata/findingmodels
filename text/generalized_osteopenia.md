# Generalized osteopenia—`OIFM_OIDM_356771`

**Synonyms:** diffuse osteopenia, generalized decreased bone density

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

Diffuse decrease in bone density throughout the visible skeleton on radiograph, suggesting osteoporosis or other metabolic bone disease.
