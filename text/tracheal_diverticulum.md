# Tracheal diverticulum—`OIFM_OIDM_554402`

**Synonyms:** diverticulum of trachea, tracheal outpouching, paratracheal air cyst

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, trachea

Outpouching of the tracheal wall, typically arising from the right posterolateral aspect of the trachea.
