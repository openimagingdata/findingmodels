# Pulmonary interstitial thickening—`OIFM_OIDM_342073`

**Synonyms:** thickening of interstitium, interstitial markings, interstitial prominence, interstitial thickening

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Thickening of the pulmonary interstitium visible on chest radiograph as reticular, reticulonodular, or linear opacities, suggesting interstitial lung disease, edema, or lymphangitic spread.
