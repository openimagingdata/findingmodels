# Abnormal thoracic kyphosis—`OIFM_OIDM_894376`

**Synonyms:** exaggerated thoracic kyphosis, increased thoracic kyphosis, thoracic hyperkyphosis

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

Exaggerated posterior curvature of the thoracic spine visible on lateral or frontal radiograph.
