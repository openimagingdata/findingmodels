# Elevation of hemidiaphragm—`OIFM_OIDM_770950`

**Synonyms:** elevated hemidiaphragm, hemidiaphragm elevation, raised hemidiaphragm, elevated diaphragm

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Abnormal superior positioning of one hemidiaphragm on chest radiograph, which may indicate phrenic nerve palsy, subpulmonic effusion, hepatomegaly, or abdominal process.
