# Cephalization of pulmonary vasculature—`OIFM_OIDM_125002`

**Synonyms:** pulmonary vascular redistribution, upper lobe pulmonary venous distension, vascular redistribution, cephalization

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Redistribution of pulmonary blood flow from the lower to the upper lobes on an upright chest radiograph, with upper lobe vessels becoming equal to or larger than lower lobe vessels, indicating elevated pulmonary venous pressure.
