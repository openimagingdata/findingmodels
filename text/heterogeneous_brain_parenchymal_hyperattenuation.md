# Heterogeneous brain parenchymal hyperattenuation—`OIFM_OIDM_865541`

**Synonyms:** brain parenchymal heterogeneous hyperattenuation, heterogeneous brain parenchymal hyperattenuation, brain parenchymal hyperdensity with heterogeneity, hyperdense heterogeneous brain parenchyma, heterogeneous hyperattenuation of brain parenchyma

CT demonstrates heterogeneous hyperattenuation within the brain parenchyma. This heterogeneity may reflect mixed attenuation densities such as acute hemorrhage, calcification, or contrast staining; correlate with timing and clinical context.
