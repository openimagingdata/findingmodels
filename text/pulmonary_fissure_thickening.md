# Pulmonary fissure thickening—`OIFM_OIDM_166982`

**Synonyms:** thickening of fissure, thickened fissure, fissural thickening, interlobar fissure thickening, fissure thickening

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased width or visibility of the pulmonary fissures on chest radiograph, often indicating fluid in the fissure (subpleural effusion) or pleural disease.
