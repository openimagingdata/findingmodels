# Pancreatic lesion—`OIFM_OIDM_371547`

**Synonyms:** pancreatic lesion, lesion of pancreas, pancreatic mass, pancreatic focal lesion, pancreatic abnormality

A focal abnormality within the pancreatic parenchyma identified on imaging. The term 'pancreatic lesion' is a non-specific descriptor that may represent cystic, solid, or inflammatory processes and often requires further imaging characterization and, when appropriate, tissue sampling for definitive diagnosis.
