# Pulmonary hyperinflation—`OIFM_OIDM_133070`

**Synonyms:** hyperinflated lungs, hyperinflation of lungs, lung hyperinflation, overinflation, hyperexpanded lungs, hyperinflation

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased lung volume on chest radiograph, manifesting as flattened diaphragms, increased AP diameter, widened intercostal spaces, and increased retrosternal clear space. Commonly seen in COPD and asthma.
