# White matter hypodensity—`OIFM_OIDM_990793`

**Synonyms:** white matter hypoattenuation, hypodense white matter, white matter low attenuation, leukoaraiosis

Regions of decreased attenuation in the cerebral white matter on CT or decreased signal on MRI. These changes can reflect chronic small vessel ischemic disease, demyelination, edema, or other pathology; the original detailed phrasing 'white matter hypodensity' exemplifies this finding.
