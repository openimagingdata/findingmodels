# Ventricular entrapment—`OIFM_OIDM_636011`

**Synonyms:** trapped ventricle, isolated ventricle

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

An isolated ventricle that is obstructed and no longer communicates freely with the remainder of the ventricular system.
