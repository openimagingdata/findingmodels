# Bronchial wall thickening—`OIFM_OIDM_961282`

**Synonyms:** peribronchial cuffing, peribronchial thickening, thickening of bronchial wall

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased thickness of the bronchial walls visible on chest radiograph, manifesting as peribronchial cuffing or tram-track opacities, often associated with inflammatory airways disease.
