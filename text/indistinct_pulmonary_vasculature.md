# Indistinct pulmonary vasculature—`OIFM_OIDM_565475`

**Synonyms:** indistinct pulmonary vessels, hazy pulmonary vasculature, perivascular haziness

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Loss of sharp definition of pulmonary vessel margins on chest radiograph, often indicating perivascular edema or interstitial disease.
