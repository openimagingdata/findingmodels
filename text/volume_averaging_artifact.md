# Volume averaging artifact—`OIFM_OIDM_671641`

**Synonyms:** partial volume effect, partial volume artifact, partial volume averaging

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Apparent image finding caused by averaging of tissues with different attenuation within a voxel or slice thickness, rather than a true discrete abnormality.
