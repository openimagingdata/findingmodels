# Abnormal hilar contour—`OIFM_OIDM_604376`

**Synonyms:** hilar abnormality, abnormal hilum, hilar contour abnormality, abnormal hila

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Abnormal size, shape, or density of the pulmonary hila on chest radiograph, which may indicate lymphadenopathy, vascular abnormality, or mass.
