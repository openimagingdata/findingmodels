# Common carotid artery stenosis—`OIFM_OIDM_490352`

**Synonyms:** CCA stenosis, common carotid artery narrowing, stenosis of the common carotid artery, CCAS, carotid stenosis (common)

Stenosis of the common carotid artery, usually due to atherosclerotic plaque, with unilateral or bilateral involvement and variable impact on cerebral perfusion. Doppler ultrasound and cross-sectional imaging characterize the degree of luminal narrowing and plaque morphology.
