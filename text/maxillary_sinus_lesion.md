# Maxillary sinus lesion—`OIFM_OIDM_479488`

**Synonyms:** maxillary sinus lesion, lesion of the maxillary sinus, maxillary sinus abnormality, paranasal sinus lesion, MS lesion

An abnormal focal lesion within the maxillary sinus detected on imaging, with radiologic characterization describing size, location, density or signal characteristics, and effects on adjacent structures; the original phrasing 'maxillary sinus lesion' illustrates this general category.
