# Beam hardening artifact—`OIFM_OIDM_727869`

**Synonyms:** posterior fossa artifact, Hounsfield bar artifact, petrous bone artifact

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

CT artifact caused by preferential absorption of lower-energy x-rays, producing streaks or dark bands near dense structures such as bone or metal.
