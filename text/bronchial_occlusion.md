# Bronchial occlusion—`OIFM_OIDM_131660`

**Synonyms:** bronchial obstruction, endobronchial obstruction, bronchial cutoff sign, bronchial cutoff

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, lung, airway

Complete or near-complete obstruction of a bronchus, which may be caused by mucus plugging, foreign body, endobronchial mass, or extrinsic compression.
