# Midline shift—`OIFM_OIDM_460241`

**Synonyms:** midline shift, midline deviation, shift of midline structures, deviation of midline structures

Displacement of cerebral hemispheres across the midsagittal plane due to mass effect or raised intracranial pressure. The falx cerebri and septum pellucidum may be displaced toward the contralateral side.
