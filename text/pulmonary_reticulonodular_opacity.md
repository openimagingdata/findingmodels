# Pulmonary reticulonodular opacity—`OIFM_OIDM_855377`

**Synonyms:** reticulonodular pattern, reticulonodular opacities, reticulonodular infiltrate, reticulonodular opacity

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, lung
