# Pulmonary architectural distortion—`OIFM_OIDM_218019`

**Synonyms:** lung architectural distortion, architectural distortion

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Distortion of normal lung architecture on chest radiograph, manifesting as abnormal displacement of bronchi, vessels, fissures, or septa, often due to fibrosis, prior surgery, or mass effect.
