# Arterial flow void—`OIFM_OIDM_352299`

**Synonyms:** arterial flow void abnormality, arterial signal void, flow void in arteries

Arterial flow void refers to the loss of signal within arteries on MRI sequences caused by rapid arterial blood flow, reflecting high-velocity flow in patent vessels. An 'arterial flow void abnormality' describes an atypical or altered flow-void appearance and may indicate vascular pathology such as stenosis or occlusion.
