# Pericardial cyst—`OIFM_OIDM_660228`

**Synonyms:** pleuropericardial cyst

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A benign fluid-filled cyst adjacent to the pericardium, typically in the right cardiophrenic angle, appearing as a well-circumscribed opacity on chest radiograph.
