# Non-included costophrenic angles—`OIFM_OIDM_646786`

**Synonyms:** costophrenic angles not included, cut off costophrenic angles, clipped costophrenic angles

**Tags:** chest, XR, technique, finding

Costophrenic angles are not included in the image field of view, limiting evaluation of the lung bases and pleural recesses.
