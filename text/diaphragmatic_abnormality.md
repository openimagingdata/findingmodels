# Diaphragmatic abnormality—`OIFM_OIDM_705569`

**Synonyms:** diaphragm abnormality, abnormal diaphragm

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Any abnormal appearance or position of the diaphragm on chest radiograph, including elevation, depression, irregularity, or loss of the normal smooth contour.
