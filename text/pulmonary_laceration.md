# Pulmonary laceration—`OIFM_OIDM_580164`

**Synonyms:** lung laceration

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, lung

Traumatic disruption of lung parenchyma, often appearing as a round or oval lucency (pneumatocele) within or adjacent to an area of contusion.
