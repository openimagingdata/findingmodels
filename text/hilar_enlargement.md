# Hilar enlargement—`OIFM_OIDM_958698`

**Synonyms:** enlarged hilum, enlarged hila, hilar prominence, prominent hila, enlargement of hilum, hilar fullness, perihilar fullness

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased size of one or both pulmonary hila on chest radiograph, which may indicate lymphadenopathy, pulmonary arterial hypertension, or mass.
