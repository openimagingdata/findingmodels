# Sinus mucosal thickening—`OIFM_OIDM_112562`

**Synonyms:** paranasal sinus mucosal thickening, mucosal thickening of the sinuses, sinus mucosal thickening, paranasal sinus mucosal thickening (PSMT)

Increase in sinus mucosal thickness on imaging, usually reflecting inflammatory or infectious mucosal reaction such as rhinosinusitis; may be associated with sinus opacification. The input term 'sinus mucosal thickening' is provided as the example use case.
