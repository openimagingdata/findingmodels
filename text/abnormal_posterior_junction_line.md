# Abnormal posterior junction line—`OIFM_OIDM_544987`

**Synonyms:** posterior junction line abnormality, abnormal posterior mediastinal line

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Deviation or abnormal appearance of the posterior junction line on chest radiograph, where the two lungs approximate behind the esophagus, which may indicate mediastinal pathology.
