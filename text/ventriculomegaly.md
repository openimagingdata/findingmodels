# Ventriculomegaly—`OIFM_OIDM_206551`

**Synonyms:** ventricular dilation, ventricular dilatation, ventricular enlargement, ventricular expansion, dilated ventricles

Ventriculomegaly denotes enlargement of the cerebral ventricles on imaging; it can reflect hydrocephalus or brain parenchymal loss. The original phrasing 'ventricular expansion' can serve as an example context for this finding.
