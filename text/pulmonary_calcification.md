# Pulmonary calcification—`OIFM_OIDM_013283`

**Synonyms:** lung calcification, calcification in lung

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Calcium deposition within the lung parenchyma visible on chest radiograph, which may be focal (granuloma, hamartoma) or diffuse (metastatic calcification, silicosis).
