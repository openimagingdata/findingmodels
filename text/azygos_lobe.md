# Azygos lobe—`OIFM_OIDM_934275`

**Synonyms:** lobus venae azygos

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

An anatomic variant in which the azygos vein courses through the apex of the right lung, creating an accessory lobe demarcated by the azygos fissure.
