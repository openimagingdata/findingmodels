# Ventriculoperitoneal shunt complication—`OIFM_OIDM_356399`

**Synonyms:** VP shunt complication, VPS complication, ventriculoperitoneal shunt malfunction, VP shunt malfunction, ventriculoperitoneal shunt apparatus complication

A ventriculoperitoneal shunt complication refers to an adverse event related to VP shunt hardware, including obstruction, disconnection, migration, fracture, infection, and overdrainage with potential pseudocyst formation. The original term 'ventriculoperitoneal shunt apparatus complication' is an example of the phrasing that would fall under this finding.
