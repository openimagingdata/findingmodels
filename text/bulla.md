# Bulla—`OIFM_OIDM_072940`

**Synonyms:** bullae, pulmonary bulla, lung bulla, cystic space

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A thin-walled, air-filled space within the lung parenchyma greater than 1 cm in diameter, typically resulting from destruction of alveolar tissue as seen in emphysema.
