# Calcified pulmonary nodule—`OIFM_OIDM_543357`

**Synonyms:** calcified granuloma, calcified lung nodule, calcified nodule

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A pulmonary nodule containing visible calcification on chest radiograph, most commonly representing a calcified granuloma from prior granulomatous infection.
