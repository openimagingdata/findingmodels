# Pulmonary oligemia—`OIFM_OIDM_229145`

**Synonyms:** decreased pulmonary vascularity, pulmonary hypoperfusion

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, lung, vascular

Decreased pulmonary vascularity in one or both lungs on chest radiograph, indicating reduced blood flow.
