# Colonic diverticulitis—`OIFM_OIDM_500879`

**Synonyms:** colornic diverticulitis, diverticulitis of the colon, diverticular inflammation of the colon

Colonic diverticulitis is inflammation or infection of colonic diverticula, most commonly involving the sigmoid colon; the original input 'colornic diverticulitis' illustrates the need to standardize terminology. On cross-sectional imaging, findings include focal colonic wall thickening with pericolonic fat stranding, and complicated disease may present with abscess, phlegmon, or perforation.
