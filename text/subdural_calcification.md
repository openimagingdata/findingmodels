# Subdural calcification—`OIFM_OIDM_975286`

**Synonyms:** subdural calcification, SDH calcification, calcified subdural lesion, calcified subdural hematoma, calcified subdural collection

Calcification within the subdural space, typically seen along the inner table of the skull on CT as radiodense densities. It often reflects a chronic subdural process or prior subdural hemorrhage with calcification; correlate with history of trauma, infection, or neurosurgical intervention.
