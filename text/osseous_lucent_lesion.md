# Osseous lucent lesion—`OIFM_OIDM_289551`

**Synonyms:** lytic lesion, bone lucency, osteolytic lesion, radiolucent bone lesion, osseous lucency

**Tags:** chest, XR, osseous, finding

An area of decreased density within bone on radiograph, which may represent a lytic lesion, cyst, or area of bone destruction.
