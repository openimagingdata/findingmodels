# Detectable internal jugular vein—`OIFM_OIDM_144470`

**Synonyms:** internal jugular vein visualization, internal jugular vein imaging, visualization of the internal jugular vein, IJV visualization, IJV imaging

**Tags:** vascular, vein, neck, internal jugular

Visualization of the internal jugular vein on an imaging study, reflecting venous patency and normal anatomical course. Lack of visualization or incomplete visualization may indicate occlusion, compression, thrombosis, or technical factors limiting assessment.
