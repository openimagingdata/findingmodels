# Arteriovenous malformation—`OIFM_OIDM_558398`

**Synonyms:** AVM, arteriovenous malformation

A congenital vascular anomaly characterized by a direct arterial-to-venous connection without an intervening capillary bed, forming a nidus. It may be intracranial or occur in other tissues and can present with hemorrhage, seizures, or neurologic deficits; radiologic hallmark is a tangle of vessels with rapid arteriovenous shunting.
