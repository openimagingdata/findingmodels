# Subdural gas—`OIFM_OIDM_436864`

**Synonyms:** subdural gas, subdural air, gas in subdural space

Gas within the subdural space is an extra-axial gas collection between the dura and arachnoid membranes. It is most commonly seen post-trauma or post-surgical and may reflect residual subdural air; correlate with history of skull fracture, dural tear, or neurosurgical intervention.
