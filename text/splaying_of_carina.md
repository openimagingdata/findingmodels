# Splaying of carina—`OIFM_OIDM_443674`

**Synonyms:** widened carinal angle, subcarinal splaying, widening of the carina

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding, mediastinum

Widening of the angle of the carina, the point where the trachea bifurcates into the main bronchi, typically indicating subcarinal mass or left atrial enlargement.
