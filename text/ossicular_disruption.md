# Ossicular disruption—`OIFM_OIDM_848829`

**Synonyms:** ossicular dislocation, ossicular chain disruption

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Discontinuity, displacement, or dislocation of the middle ear ossicular chain visible on imaging, often related to trauma, erosion, or prior surgery.
