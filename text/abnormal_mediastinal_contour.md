# Abnormal mediastinal contour—`OIFM_OIDM_103158`

**Synonyms:** mediastinal contour abnormality, abnormal mediastinal silhouette

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Abnormal widening, irregularity, or asymmetry of the mediastinal silhouette on chest radiograph, suggesting mediastinal mass, lymphadenopathy, or vascular abnormality.
