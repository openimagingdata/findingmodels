# Emphysema—`OIFM_OIDM_002082`

**Synonyms:** emphysematous changes, emphysematous lung disease

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Permanent enlargement of airspaces distal to the terminal bronchioles, manifesting on chest radiograph as hyperinflation, increased lucency, and flattened hemidiaphragms.
