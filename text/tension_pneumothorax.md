# Tension pneumothorax—`OIFM_OIDM_440635`

**Synonyms:** tension pneumo

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

A life-threatening pneumothorax with progressive air accumulation causing mediastinal shift to the contralateral side, flattening or inversion of the ipsilateral hemidiaphragm, and hemodynamic compromise.
