# Aortic tortuosity—`OIFM_OIDM_517028`

**Synonyms:** tortuosity of aorta, tortuous aorta, aortic elongation, unfolding of aorta, unfolded aorta, aortic unfolding

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Increased tortuosity or winding course of the aorta on chest radiograph, commonly seen with aging, hypertension, and atherosclerosis.
