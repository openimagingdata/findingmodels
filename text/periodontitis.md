# Periodontitis—`OIFM_OIDM_263308`

**Synonyms:** periodontal disease, chronic periodontitis

**Tags:** head, CT, finding

Inflammatory disease of the periodontal tissues that can produce alveolar bone loss, periodontal ligament widening, or other dental supporting-structure changes on imaging.
