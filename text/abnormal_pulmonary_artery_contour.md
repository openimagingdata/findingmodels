# Abnormal pulmonary artery contour—`OIFM_OIDM_792655`

**Synonyms:** pulmonary artery contour abnormality, abnormal main pulmonary artery

**Tags:** chest, XR, finding

Abnormal prominence or contour of the main pulmonary artery segment on chest radiograph, which may indicate pulmonary hypertension, pulmonary artery dilation, or congenital anomaly.
